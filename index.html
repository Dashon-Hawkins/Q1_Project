Software Development

To work with focus, instead of reactively, start your day by deciding the three things you want to get done each day. you have some time at the beginning of each day before all the distractions come pouring in. Using that time to decide what you want to accomplish during the day can set your course. A better way to approach your productivity is that productivity isn’t about doing more things — it’s about doing the right things.

User Experience Focused

By utilizing elements and principles of the Pomodor Technique, Agile software development, Scrum, GTD (Getting Things Done) and Iterative and incremental development. We took a more health focused approach to the idea. Focusing on incorporating help tips during the rest periods such as activities and exercises (see below) in order to promote a "anti-sedentary" lifestyle while working and included are tips that rest you mind and eyes from the computer screen.

Easy to work with

There are always plenty of good things to cross-off your to-do list, but superior productivity requires tackling the best things first. The stuff that has the greatest leverage and ROI in moving you closer to your goals. Of course, there may be some times where you need to divert your attention for something worthwhile. However, most of your daily interruptions don’t fall into this category. Decide what’s important before someone hijacks your time and it’ll be easier to tell when something really does need to take precedence.
